About Us

Meet the CEO

The creative mind behind Aurex Designs Studio

Founder & CEO

Mustapha Oniyangi

Creative Director & Lead Designer

With over 5 years of experience in the creative industry, I founded Aurex Designs with a vision to transform how brands communicate visually. Based in Ilorin, Nigeria, I've had the privilege of working with clients across Africa and beyond, helping them build memorable brand identities that resonate with their audiences.

My approach combines strategic thinking with artistic excellence, ensuring every design not only looks stunning but also achieves business objectives. From startups to established enterprises, I bring the same level of passion and dedication to every project.
